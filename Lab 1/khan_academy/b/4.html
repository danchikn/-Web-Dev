Travel to China with us

China, a magnificent country with a rich cultural heritage and spectacular natural attractions, offers a unique experience for tourists.
In this country you will find a mixture of centuries-old history, the modern world and the impressive beauty of nature. Our journey starts from the capital of China Beijing.

Beijing:

Welcome to the capital of China, ancient and modern Beijing! Here you will find the majestic walls of the Chinese Kremlin, the delightful palaces of the Forbidden City and the mystical atmosphere of the temples of the Heavenly Temple. Experience the spirit of antiquity on Gulu Street, where ancient temples and modern boutiques are side by side. Treat yourself to an unforgettable evening at the famous Beijing Theater while enjoying classic Chinese performances.

Shanghai:

Welcome to the metropolis of the future, Shanghai! Glass skyscrapers and traditional Chinese palaces have merged here. Take a walk along the Western Band promenade and feel the breath of the metropolis, enjoying the view of the Pearl Tower and the endless lights of the night city. Immerse yourself in the world of art on the Bund embankment and feel inspired in the historic quarter of the French Concession.

Pross and Cons

Of course, every country has its drawbacks, but despite this, many people like to travel and explore the world.

Positive:

HELLO

World

  • Rich cultural heritage: China has a rich history and culture, offering tourists a unique opportunity to immerse themselves in centuries-old traditions, visit ancient temples, monasteries and monuments such as the Great Wall of China and the Forbidden City.
  • A variety of natural attractions: From majestic mountains to fertile valleys, from snow-capped peaks to picturesque waterfalls, China offers a variety of natural landscapes that amaze the imagination and call for exploration.
  • Innovation and modernity: As one of the world's leading economies, China also attracts tourists with its modern achievements. This is a country where smartphones and electric vehicles coexist with ancient pagodas, creating a unique atmosphere of combining old and new.

Minuses

  • Language barrier: Chinese is one of the most difficult languages to learn, which can make it difficult to communicate with locals, especially in rural areas and outside tourist areas.
  • Mass tourism: Some popular tourist attractions, such as the Great Wall of China and the Forbidden City, can be crowded with tourists, especially during peak seasons, which reduces the pleasure of visiting.
  • Environmental issues: Some regions of China face serious environmental problems such as air and water pollution, which can negatively affect the experience of tourists and cause concern due to the environmental impact.

Hangzhou:

Welcome to the peaceful beauty of Hangzhou, the city of gardens and lakes! Discover the historic Lingyan Temple and the amazing Tiger Hill, take a look at the huge Xilin Lake and take a walk along the picturesque Western Lake Westlake National Park and immerse yourself in Chinese culture by visiting the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Historical Museum.

Tianjin:

Welcome to the modern metropolis with a historical heritage, Tianjin! Feel the pulse of the city as you walk around Tiananmen Square and explore the old streets of the city. Spend the day shopping and dining on the famous Anli Street, and then enjoy the sunset on the banks of the Haihe River . Feel the atmosphere of antiquity by walking through the historical area of the Italian Concession and explore the unique architecture of the international concession.

Cuisine

Cultural gourmets will appreciate the richness of Chinese cuisine, where each province offers its own unique tastes and traditions. From classic dishes from Beijing flavors of the Sichuan region, China will delight your taste buds.

Conclusion

China is a place where modernity meets tradition, where Oriental mysticism and modern achievements are intertwined into a unique experience for anyone who decides to explore this amazing country.
