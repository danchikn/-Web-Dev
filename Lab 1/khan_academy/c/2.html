One-Pot Chicken and Rice

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: hot
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 1 tablespoon
skinless chicken
breasts		 4 boneless
uncooked rice 1 cup
chicken broth 2 cups
garlic powder 1 teaspoon
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Season chicken breasts with garlic powder, salt, and pepper, then add them to the skillet. Cook until browned on both sides, about 4-5 minutes per side.
  • Remove chicken from skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add uncooked rice and chicken broth. Stir to combine.
  • Place chicken breasts on top of the rice mixture. Add diced vegetables around the chicken.
  • Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until rice is cooked and chicken is cooked through.
  • Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.

Classic Caesar Salad

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: immediately
Ingredients Quantity
romaine lettuce 1 head
croutons 4 1/2 cup
Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup grated
Caesar salad 1 bunch

Instructions:

  • In a large bowl, combine chopped romaine lettuce and croutons.
  • Add Caesar salad dressing to taste and toss until the lettuce and croutons are evenly coated.
  • Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  • Serve immediately as a side dish or add grilled chicken for a complete meal.

No-Bake Chocolate
Peanut Butter Bars

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: after 2 hr
Ingredients Quantity
peanut butter 1 cup
honey 1/2 cup
coconut oil 1/2 cup
rolled oats 2 cups
rolled oats 2 cups
cocoa powder 1/4 cup
vanilla extract 1 teaspoon

Instructions:

  • In a saucepan over low heat, melt together peanut butter, honey, and coconut oil, stirring until smooth.
  • Remove from heat and stir in rolled oats, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until well combined.
  • Press the mixture into an 8x8 inch pan lined with parchment paper.
  • Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until set.
  • Cut into bars and serve chilled.
